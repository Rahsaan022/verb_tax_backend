Is the verb:

1) Communicatively Targeted? Does the verb's execution communicate to another (like a command or wave)?

2) Externally Perceptible? Can someone else see it, like "run" or is it internal like "become upset?"

3) in the Present? Is the verb happening here and now? Not past, future, or hypothetical?

4) describing reality, or Actual? Does the verb describe "what is," not what "should" or "could be?"

5) describing a State? As in "I know Spanish," "He has one," not an experience like "I studied Spanish," "I will take one."

6) Agentive? Does occur from someone's will or volition? Eg., walking, jumping vs falling or aging.
